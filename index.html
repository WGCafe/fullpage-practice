The founder Walter Wang Yue is a veteran property management guru for the shared economy. He was an Airbnb host with over 200 reviews since 2013. He had hosted more than 500 guests from 100 cities, making friends from all over the world. To solve the issues of check-in,

Product

Popular vacation rental or home sharing platforms were establish trust by being the dependable middleman to connect the owner and guest. Decentralized platform using the Blockchain technology is able connect owner and guest with transparency, fairness and trust with out the need for middlemen and the hefty transaction fees they charge.
